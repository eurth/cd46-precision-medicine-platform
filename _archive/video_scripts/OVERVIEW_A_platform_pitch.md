"These are the headline scientific findings. Three to focus on.

First: prostate adenocarcinoma, ovarian cancer, and bladder cancer are the
top-priority indications — established by combining expression level,
protein evidence, CRISPR functional data, and survival impact.

Second: approximately 35% of metastatic castration-resistant prostate cancer
patients who are PSMA-low — the patients who do not respond to Pluvicto,

Third: androgen receptor blockade — enzalutamide, abiraterone — upregulates
CD46 expression. Resistance to standard-of-care simultaneously makes patients
better candidates for 225Ac-CD46 therapy. Treatment resistance is a patient
enrichment mechanism."
